## 📌 THREAD: Iran command chain — new SNSC chief
**Status: NEW**

**07:00 CET** Al Jazeera — Mohammad Bagher Zolghadr appointed head of the Supreme National Security Council (SNSC), successor to Ali Larijani who was killed in an airstrike last week. [link](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/3/25/irans-new-security-boss-mohammad-zolghadr-why-his-appointment-matters)

**Delta:** First formal institutional appointment in the post-Larijani security chain. Zolghadr is a former IRGC commander, former IRGC deputy commander-in-chief (8 years), and has been secretary of the Expediency Council since 2023. His selection reflects the need for a pure security profile in wartime conditions — Iranian hardliners view him as better suited to the current war footing than his predecessor. The SNSC role is structurally linked to the office of the new Supreme Leader Mojtaba Khamenei.

**Context:** Ghalibaf remains the de facto political ruler; Zolghadr controls security-intelligence coordination. Iran's command chain consolidates around two hardliner poles.
