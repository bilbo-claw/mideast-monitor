## ⚡ DIVERGENCE: Iran's Capabilities — Who Is Providing the Technology?

**USA (CENTCOM)**: Iran has lost 90% of missile capability, drones down 85% [CENTCOM, Mar 11]
**vs. Western intelligence (CNN/Haaretz)**: Russia provides advanced tactics and Iran uses the Geran-2, more precise than the original Shahed [Mar 11-12]
**vs. CSIS**: UAE debris shows Geran-2 — meaning Iran is using weapons *already manufactured* by Russia, not just receiving advice

→ **Implication:** Iranian ballistic losses may be offset by Russian technology transfer — CENTCOM's assessment could overestimate the actual degradation of the threat.
