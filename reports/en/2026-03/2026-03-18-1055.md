**Status: NEW**

[07:56 CET] NYT — Israel hopes the bombings will trigger a **popular uprising and regime change** in Iran. Netanyahu convinced that striking leadership figures can trigger an internal collapse. [NYT](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/03/18/world/middleeast/israel-strikes-iran-war-regime-change.html)

**Counter-narrative (ISW, Mar 16):** The IRGC believes, on the contrary, that the conflict is **unifying** the Iranian population around the regime, as the Iran-Iraq War did in the 1980s. President Pezeshkian warned the IRGC that planning economic recovery based on this assumption is dangerous.

⚡ **STRATEGIC DIVERGENCE:** Netanyahu-Trump are betting on Iranian internal collapse; the IRGC is using the war as a national cohesion tool. The two logics are mutually exclusive — and determine whether there will be a negotiated exit.
