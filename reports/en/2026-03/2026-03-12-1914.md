## 📌 THREAD: Mojtaba Khamenei — First Statement

**Status: NEW — ESCALATION**

**18:05 CET** — AP/NYT/Guardian — Ayatollah Mojtaba Khamenei issues his **first public statement** since inheriting Iran's supreme leadership. He has not appeared in public since late February (reportedly wounded in the opening strike). The message is read by a state TV anchor.
[AP](https://apnews.com/article/iran-israel-us-march-12-2026-oil-prices-90e17dbf7354d1e9428994ab2a036506) | [Guardian](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2026/mar/12/iran-vows-to-fight-on-in-first-message-issued-in-name-of-mojtaba-khamenei) | [NYT](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/03/12/world/middleeast/khamenei-statement.html)

**Key content:**
- Hormuz **must remain closed** as a "tool of pressure on the enemy"
- Iran **will continue attacking US bases** in the region
- Promise to **avenge the blood of martyrs** (including 165+ killed in Minab school)
- Iran will attack **US bases in the Gulf** if not closed

**Delta:** First direct signal from Khamenei Jr. on strategic direction. No negotiating opening. Hard line confirmed despite 13 days of bombardment.

Storage tanks hit in Bahrain and Oman. Tehran warns: prepare for **oil at $200**. Any tanker bound for the US, Israel or partners is "a legitimate target."
[Guardian](https://www.theguardian.com/business/2026/mar/12/middle-east-war-creating-largest-supply-disruption-in-the-history-of-oil-markets) | [NYT-IEA](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/03/12/world/middleeast/iran-war-oil-iea.html)

**Delta:** Iraq — previously on the sidelines — is directly caught in the energy spiral for the first time. Basra terminal closure = reduced Iraqi export flow. Risk spreading to non-belligerent countries.

## 📌 THREAD: Pezeshkian — Diplomatic Off-Ramp?

**Status: NEW — DE-ESCALATION (weak signal)**

**15:41 CET** — Al Jazeera — Iranian President Masoud Pezeshkian posts on X the **terms for ending the war**: (1) recognition of Iran's legitimate rights, (2) payment of reparations, (3) international guarantees against future aggression. Says he spoke with leaders of **Russia and Pakistan** and confirmed "Iran's commitment to peace."
[Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/3/12/irans-president-sets-terms-to-end-the-war-is-an-off-ramp-in-sight)

**Delta:** First public formulation of exit conditions from Tehran in 13 days. Analysts: Iran is trying to shape the "post-war" framework before negotiating. Pezeshkian is politically more moderate than Khamenei Jr.

⚡ **DIVERGENCE: Khamenei Jr. vs Pezeshkian**
- Mojtaba Khamenei (18:05 CET, state TV) — *"Hormuz must stay closed, we will continue attacks, we will avenge the martyrs"*
- Pezeshkian (15:41 CET, X) — *"Iran is committed to peace, here are the terms"*
→ **Implication:** Role division or real fracture? The supreme leader dictates the war, the president builds the diplomatic narrative for future talks. Watch: the Pezeshkian-Russia-Pakistan channel may be the real negotiation path.
